Seasong — Trusted Used & Refurbished Smartphones, Delivered Globally.

Seasong is a Hong Kong–based company specializing in the sourcing, inspection, and distribution of used and refurbished smartphones for global markets.

With Hong Kong’s strategic position as an international trade and logistics hub, Seasong connects reliable supply chains with partners and customers across Asia, Europe, and other regions. We focus on delivering market-ready devices that meet strict quality, functionality, and compliance standards.

Every smartphone handled by Seasong goes through a standardized process including professional inspection, functional testing, grading, and secure data erasure. Our goal is to ensure transparency, consistency, and trust at every stage—from procurement to delivery.

We work closely with wholesalers, retailers, e-commerce sellers, and distribution partners, offering flexible solutions for bulk orders, mixed models, and customized requirements. Whether for resale, refurbishment programs, or secondary markets, Seasong provides dependable support backed by operational experience and industry knowledge.

Committed to sustainability and responsible reuse, Seasong believes that extending the lifecycle of smartphones not only creates economic value, but also contributes to reducing electronic waste and promoting a more circular global economy.
